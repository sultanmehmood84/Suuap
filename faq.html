Freuently Asked Questions

General FAQ:

Why is this app better than other platforms?
This app lets you buy and sell conveniently and securely among your peers from the same college. Everything is classified properly so it’s easier to search.
Do I have to pay to use the app?
It's free !
Why should I use the app?
Safety: You only buy and sell with students from your school. No third party people involved.

Convenient: No need to package and ship items.
Who are the users?
All users are current attending students just like you.

Seller FAQ:

How do I deliver the items?
You will be able to contact the buyer through the app to meet up on campus for delivery.
Is there a limit on how many posts I can post per month?
There is no limit. You can post and sell as many as you want every month.

Buyer FAQ:

How do I return an item?
Unfortunately we are only a marketplace that provides service for peers to exchange. We do not sell or own any of the listed items so we do not accept any returns.
How will the item be shipped? How do I track the shipment?
You’ll be able to contact your peers once you have purchased for meetup location.
